Your company's memory, made operable.

Company Brain rebuilds your company's knowledge as a graph you can read, edit, and operate. The context that lives in people's heads, scattered docs, and old threads becomes one connected asset your team and your AI can actually use. Not a black box.

Scattered knowledge, rebuilt as a graph.

How it works

  • It maps where your knowledge actually lives, across docs, threads, tools, and the heads of the people who hold it.
  • It rebuilds that into a connected graph, structured and navigable, instead of a pile of files nobody can find.
  • You read it, edit it, and operate it. Your AI queries the graph instead of guessing, so it stops getting your company wrong.
